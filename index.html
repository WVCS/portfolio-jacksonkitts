Types of Surfboards

There are many types of surfboards that people use. There are 3 main categories of Surfboards; Longboards, Shortboards, and Funboards. Although, there are many subcategories of each style. Each Board is designed the way it is for a specific type of surfing. For example, beginners would find it much easier to ride a longboard, because it is easy to keep your balance and easy to catch waves with a longer board, but professional surfers almost alwats prefer shortboards, because they are lighter and easier to turn and do tricks with.

Above is a couple different types of boards that people buy. A fish board, recognized by its inverted tail, is very popular among experienced surfers who like shortboards.
